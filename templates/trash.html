NON-RECYCLED WASTE



Non-recycled waste is a major contributor to greenhouse gas emissions, particularly carbon dioxide (CO2). When waste is sent to landfills, it decomposes anaerobically, releasing methane, a potent greenhouse gas. The production and transportation of goods also generate CO2 emissions. Recycling helps reduce these emissions by diverting waste from landfills and conserving resources.
